Unity Discovery - 14/10/2015

Was looking around for a way to switch materials within script and found out its quite simple. Had a play around with it and put together a piece of code that that randomly assigns a material to an object. I created an array of materials and then using the objects renderer I just set the material through selecting a random index from the array. This will be handy for adding a bit of variety into Beer Run.
